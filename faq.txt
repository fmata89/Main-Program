
                                    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

- Is the App free of charge?
        Yes, the app and all of its services are free to use.

- Who owns the ATP Player Information?
        The ATP owns all the player information displayed on the app. They are also the source for its validity.

- Where can I find the App's latest Version Updates?
        Under the main menu. Select the option Learn About and go to the "Updates" section inside the document.
